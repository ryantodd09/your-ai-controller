What this is

A weekly, practical guide to using AI in regulatory reporting, accounting, and finance.

Each issue gives you one idea you can use this week, the guardrails that keep it safe in a highly regulated environment, and an honest take on where AI provides the best benefit for you and your team - the same version I give to my team today.

This is a passion project. I'm an accountant by trade with a regulatory reporting focus - a full time career with a young family to boot.

Who writes it

Accounting clicked with me right away - I took an entry level college course while still in high school that focused on budgeting, bookkeeping, and accounting basics - the difference between a debit and a credit. And I immediately realized this would be my career.

After earning my CPA, I spent ten years in public accounting, leading engagement teams responsible for integrated audits of large filers. This taught me the importance of relationship and team building, project and time management, and grit - good ol' fashioned hard work.

I've spent the latter half of my career in regulatory reporting, leveraging those skills to accelerate my career growth in the corporate world. AI has been the next big challenge and opportunity - where I've focused my efforts. I've learned where it earns its keep and where it doesn't belong.

This is the practical guidance I'd hand a teammate. Built on my hard-won experience.
