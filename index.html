The shift

AI shouldn't replace you. It should move you up.

The grind - GL and interseries report reconciliations, crosschecking, summarizing logic, comparing results - is exactly what AI is good at. Your judgment, your read of the instructions, and your sign-off are exactly what it isn't. Done right, AI takes the busywork and you move into review: managing the output, applying judgment, owning the controls - a human in the loop. Every step it takes should be one you can see, check, and defend.
